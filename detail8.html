Yamaha BB424 VW Electric Bass

Inheriting the sound and design of theBB2000 series basses it makes for an excellent entry-level instrument for up and coming Rock bassists. The lineup includes both 4-and 5-string models. In Diagonal Body Thru Stringing, strings are angled 45 degree at the saddle to reduce the stress and secure their tension. Moreover, because the attachment area of strings to saddles is larger than conventional vertical stringing, much better transmission of vibration is obtained. Specifications : Scale Length 34" (863.6mm) Fingerborad Rosewood Body Alder Neck Maple/ Nato 5pcs Pickups Split Single x 1, Bar Single
